Alienware Bot

Respect my authoritah!

AWB0t

Alienware Bot has two functions: prevent raffle commands spam (ModBot) and collect raffle winners (RaffleBot).

ModBot

ModBot times out people who spam the raffle commands (!ticket and commands for keyword raffles). Any raffle command outside of raffles will be deleted to prevent further spam. If a raffle is open, only repeated entries that are valid will be deleted. Invalid !ticket commands (e.g. "!ticket", "!ticket 9001", "!ticket all") and "!ticket 0" will be ignored, so people won't get punished for correcting their typos or leaving the raffle.

ModBot has a two level system to punish the raffle commands spam. The system is similar to the cards in a soccer game:

  1. yellow card = 1 second timeout (default)
  2. red card = 10 minutes timeout (default)

Further spam will be punished with additional red cards. Whenever a raffle starts or ends, the cards are reset and new offenders are punished with a yellow card.

The commands to control ModBot are:

  • !awmodbot – shows current version and state of ModBot
  • !awmodbot on – enables ModBot
  • !awmodbot off – disables ModBot
  • !awmodbot raffle – shows ModBot's raffle state
  • !awmodbot raffle on – manually sets ModBot's raffle state to ON (time out multiple raffle commands)
  • !awmodbot raffle off – manually sets ModBot's raffle state to OFF (time out all raffle commands)
  • !yellowcard – shows timeout duration of yellow cards
  • !yellowcard <time> – sets timeout duration of yellow cards to <time> seconds
  • !redcard – shows timeout duration of red cards
  • !redcard <time> – sets timeout duration of red cards to <time> seconds
  • !checkcard <account> – shows how many cards <account> has
  • !resetcards – manually resets cards
